Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1. How do I borrow books from the library

To borrow books, visit the library's circulation desk, present your library card, and the librarian will assist you in checking out your desired books.

Q2: Can I renew my borrowed books?

Yes, in most cases, you can renew books online through our website or by visiting the library. Renewal periods may vary, so please check the due date and library policies.

Q3: What is the library's Wi-Fi password?

The library's Wi-Fi password is available at the circulation desk. Feel free to ask library staff for assistance if you need help connecting to our Wi-Fi network.

Q4: How can I reserve a meeting room in the library?

You can reserve a meeting room by contacting the library's administrative office. Reservation details, including availability and policies, can be obtained by calling our main office.

Q5: Can I access e-books and digital resources from home?

Yes, the library offers access to a variety of e-books and digital resources. Visit our website and log in with your library card to explore our online collection.

Q6: What do I do if I've lost my library card?

If you've lost your library card, please visit the circulation desk as soon as possible to report it. We'll help you replace your card and ensure the security of your account.

Q7: Does the library offer printing and photocopying services?

Yes, the library provides printing and photocopying services. Ask library staff for assistance, and they will guide you through the process and inform you of any associated fees.

Q8: Are there any late fees for overdue books?

We welcome book suggestions from our patrons. You can recommend a book by filling out a suggestion form available at the circulation desk or by submitting an online request through our website.

Q9: What events and programs does the library offer?

The library hosts a variety of events and programs, including book clubs, author talks, and workshops. Check our event calendar on the website or inquire at the library for upcoming activities.
